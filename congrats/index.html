The following are some ideas for the new Django "It Worked" page.

The general idea of these "mock-ups" is to have a simple, clean design with an illustration/animation of Django's namesake, guitarist Django Reinhardt.

The font (Roboto) and colors of the mock-ups attempt to reflect the design of the https://www.djangoproject.com/ website. The Django Animation links to a wikipedia article on Reinhardt.

Also the animation is copyright Cornelius Joksch (http://www.corneliusjoksch.de/). He has said that we can use his animation of Django free of charge as long as we can come to a proper agreement regarding attribution on the "It works" page. One idea is to put a credit directly under the animation (which I've done in the mockups). Again, though promising, we are still in talks, so no guarantee we can use his animation just yet.

Note: The CSS is being created "mock-up" style. Meaning attention is solely on the design output and not on the code quality during this stage of design "trial and error."
Eventually everything will be inlined and embedded in the HTML, so the page doesn't need to call any resources. For example, for these mockups the animation was converted to a data:uri and the Django logo is an svg.
